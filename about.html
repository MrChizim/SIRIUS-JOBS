Our Story

Sirius Jobs is a registered Nigerian company established in 2022, dedicated to connecting verified professionals with individuals and businesses across multiple sectors. We operate as a comprehensive platform bridging talent and opportunity in Nigeria.

Our platform serves diverse needs: skilled artisans (electricians, plumbers, cleaners), verified professionals (doctors, lawyers, therapists), job seekers and employers, marketplace vendors, and specialized services like day Personal Assistants. We provide trusted connections where professionals can reach customers quickly and efficiently.

Our mission is to simplify access to quality services while creating sustainable opportunities across Nigeria. From finding a reliable electrician to booking an affordable doctor consultation, from hiring full-time staff to getting a PA for a day—Sirius Jobs is your trusted partner.

Our vision is clear: to become Nigeria's most comprehensive service-matching platform, empowering professionals, building trust, and delivering excellent experiences to every home and organization.

Reliable people. Trusted service. Proven results.

Chizim V. Felix

Scientist, Biochemist & Innovator

His leadership combines scientific precision with a deep understanding of real-world employment challenges. Through Sirius Jobs, Chizim has built a platform that connects talent with opportunity, emphasizing integrity, transparency, and excellence.

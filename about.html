Our Story

Sirius Jobs is a registered Nigerian company established in 2022, dedicated to connecting verified blue-collar workers with individuals and businesses that need reliable services.

We provide a trusted platform where skilled professionals such as electricians, plumbers, cleaners, and artisans can reach customers quickly and efficiently. Our mission is to simplify access to quality service while creating sustainable job opportunities across Nigeria.

Our vision is clear: to become a nationwide network that empowers skilled workers, builds trust, and delivers excellent service experiences to every home and organization.

Reliable people. Trusted service. Proven results.

Meet Our Founder

The visionary behind Sirius Jobs

Founder & CEO

Chizim V. Felix

Scientist, Biochemist & Innovator

Chizim Felix is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Sirius Jobs. He is a dedicated scientist and biochemist from Uppsala University, Sweden, with a strong passion for innovation, research, and technology-driven solutions.

His leadership combines scientific precision with a deep understanding of real-world employment challenges. Through Sirius Jobs, Chizim has built a platform that connects talent with opportunity, emphasizing integrity, transparency, and excellence.

His vision is to empower individuals by bridging the gap between skills and demand, while promoting global collaboration and career growth.

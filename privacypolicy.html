At Sirius Jobs, your privacy and trust are our top priorities. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Personal information you provide when registering (name, email, phone, ID details).
  • Employment data (job history, applications, employer feedback).
  • Payment information through secure payment gateways (we never store card numbers).
  • Verification data such as NIN, Driver's License, or Passport numbers and related documents.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Verify your identity and eligibility for employment.
  • Match job seekers with employers.
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • Improve and personalize user experience.
  • Communicate updates, offers, and system notifications.

3. Data Protection

  • We store all sensitive data in encrypted form.
  • Access to verification documents is restricted to authorized administrators only.
  • We do not sell or share your information with unauthorized third parties.
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill verification and legal requirements.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted partners such as Paystack, Flutterwave, and KYC verification providers. These partners follow strict data protection and compliance policies.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or update your information.
  • Request deletion of your account.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communication.
  • Contact us for data-related questions at siriusoddjobs@gmail.com.

6. NDPR Compliance

Sirius Jobs adheres to the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR). We ensure lawful processing, transparency, and security in all personal data handling.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

Questions about your data?

Contact our privacy team for any data-related inquiries.
